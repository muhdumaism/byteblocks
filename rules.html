Discord server

  1. Be respectful to your peers.
  2. No harassment, bullying, slurs or aggressive behaviour.
  3. There is a zero-tolerance policy for racist, sexist, homophobic, or otherwise prejudiced comments, including disrespectful remarks towards race/religion/political beliefs.
  4. No trolling, baiting, discussions in ill faith, posting for shock value, etc.
  5. No spamming (in-text or on voice call) or screaming at others in voice chat.
  6. If there is background noise, you must use push to talk. Deliberately disrupting study rooms will result in an immediate ban.
  7. No explicit NSFW content. i.e., p*rn, g*re, etc. This includes in-text, using a music bot, attachments/links sent, and profile pictures.
  8. If you wish to submit content and you are not sure whether it is in violation of this rule, please feel free to PM anyone with the role of “Moderator,” and we can verify for you.
